Use this process to extract the majority of coding standards before leaving

1) In Confluence > Advanced Details > View Source
2) Copy the <body></body> tag and all contents
3) Use Turndown to convert HTML to Markdown and remove all rubbish
4) Use Dillinger to read/parse Markdown and edit
